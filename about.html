About the League

The Larchmont Basketball League is a prestigious annual basketball league in Larchmont NY founded by Matt Orchanian and Lucas Ierodiaconou. Every year teams go out and fight to win the honor of being champion. This is the third year of the league which was founded in 2019.

The tournament takes place in Sheldrake park, often referred to as the Mecca of Westchester Basketball, where the best hoopers from the 914 come to display their skills. The two previous winners of this coveted trophy were Nick Orchanians' team in 2019 and Lucas Ierodiaconou's Team Black in 2020. In 2020 Myles Fleming won MVP, Lucas Ierodiaconou won COTY, and Davey Kaplan won MIP.
